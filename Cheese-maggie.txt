Recipe from Rajdeep

 Boil the maggie
 Separate the maggie from the boiled water to a different bowl.
 In an empty pan put a slice of cheese to fry the maggie.
 Put the boiled maggie into the pan and fry with the cheese for 1min untill all the cheese mix up with maggie.
 Pour the boiled water into the pan and add the masala to it.
 Cook untill all the masala mixes up well.
 Add some chillies for more spicy.
 Add some shredded cheese to garnish.
 
 You have your dish ready.
