//Follow Steps carefully to make a good and tasty tea
1.  Take about 1 cup of water (for each person)
2.  Add around 1 teaspoon full of tea.
3.  Add 1 teaspoon full of sugar per cup.
4.  Add 1/4th milk of total water added initially.
5.  Let it boil for 5 minutes.

    TEA IS NOW READY TO SERVE ENJOY IT......
