Dessert is a course that concludes a meal. The course consists of sweet foods, such as candy, and possibly
a beverage such as dessert wine and liqueur


Desserts are sweet and often indulgent treats that are typically served at the end of a meal or as a standalone delight. They come in a wide variety of flavors, textures, and styles, catering to a diverse range of preferences. Desserts can be simple and comforting, or they can be elaborate and artistic creations that showcase the culinary skills of the chef.
