I am sharing the receipe of Momos here:

for stuffing:
  onion 
  carrot
  cabbage
  green chillies
  
for exterior coating:
  maida
 

cut the vegetables fines and mesh them
make maida(all purpose flour) and make dough
roll the dough in small round shapes and fill the  filling of vegies and close them
steam them up and eat with you fav green chutney or red sauce
