# Pasta recipe

1. First boil the pasta.
2. Create white sauce with milk, flour, origano, salt, butter and some chilli flakes.
3. Chop some veggies of your choices.
4. Add oil in the pan and fry the veggies with the boiled pasta.
5. Add some salt.
6. Finally add the white sauce which we have prepared and let it cook at medium flame for 5-10 min.
7. Ta-da! our delicious pasta is ready.
