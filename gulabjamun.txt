1. Get gulab jamun flour,oil and sugar.
2. prepare small spheres with gulabjamun dough.
3. Fry those spherical items in oil pan.
4. Take another pan put some water and sugar boil for some time.
5. The fried spherical items put it in bioled sugar water.
6. Wait for some time to obsorb the sugar water into item.
7. Now gulab jamun is ready.
