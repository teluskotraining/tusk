you can make burgers in 4 steps!

1. Make the burger patties – by steaming & boiling the veggies and then sauteing them with spices

2. Fry the patties – make a batter for frying the burger patties
3. Prepare the burger sauce – make an easy sauce to add to the burgers

4. Assemble the burgers – with add-ins like tomato, cucumber, onion & lettuce!

It might seem like only 4 steps but it takes some time to put it all together!
