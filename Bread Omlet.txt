Step 1 : Take a Dosa pan heat it with mild oil 
Step 2 : Take the two eggs mix nicely 
Step 3 : Pour on pan and spread all around 
Step 4 : Take two Bread slices put it on Egg spread
Step 5 : Take some Mayonnaise and pudhina chutney spread on bread slices
Step 6 : Fold the Egg spread on Bread slices cut it by 6 pieces
Step 7 : Serve with Tomato Sauce and Mayonnaise.

Need Ingredients : Bread,2-Eggs,salt,Mayonnaise,oil,Pudhina chutney Tomato Sauce.
