



Recipe from Y.Perera

Ingredients
    3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
    6 uncooked lasagna noodles
    1 cube chicken bouillon
    Â¼ cup hot water
    1  package cream cheese, softened
    2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    1 jar spaghetti sauce

step 1: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. 
        Cook lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes.
        Drain, rinse with cold water, and set aside.

step 2: Meanwhile, place the chicken in a saucepan with enough water to cover, and bring to a boil.
        Cook for 20 minutes, or until no longer pink and juices run clear.
        Remove from saucepan, and shred
        
step 3: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
        Dissolve the bouillon cube in hot water.
        In a large bowl, mix the chicken with the bouillon, cream cheese, and 1 cup mozzarella cheese.  
        
step 4: Spread 1/3 of spaghetti sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
        Cover with the chicken mixture, and top with 3 lasagna noodles; repeat.
        Top with remaining sauce, and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese.
        
step 5: Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven.
