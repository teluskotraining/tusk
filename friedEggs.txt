// Recipe from Rodgers Tobiko

Ingredients and necessary utensils: Eggs, cooking oil,salt,bowl,frying pan,spoon.
step 1 : Crack your eggs into the bowl
step 2 : Add a pinch of salt and stir to scramble the eggs
step 3 : Place your frying pan to the fire and put two to two and a half spoons of
         cooking oil and heat it abit
step 4 : Pour your scrambled mixture onto the pan and wait until its solid and 
         flip  it to cook the other side.Keep checking so as not to overcook it
step 5 : Serve your eggs and unleash your hunger on them
