Step 1

Mix the ginger garlic paste, 1/2 tsp red chilli powder, corn flour, salt and lemon juice in a bowl. Add a little water and mix wel.
Step 2

Add the mushrooms and mix until evenly coated with the spices.
Step 3

Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium flame. Fry the mushrooms until golden brown and crisp. Remove and drain excess oil.
Step 4

Heat little oil in an another pan. Saute the ginger, garlic, green chillies, onions and curry leaves for a minute or two.
Step 5

Add the red chilli powder and fried mushrooms. Stir well and cook for another minute.
Step 6

Garnish with coriander leaves and spring onions. Serve with chutney or sauce.
