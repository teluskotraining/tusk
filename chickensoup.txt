Preparing Chicken and Asparagus soup

Step - 1
=========

Combine minced chicken and chopped onion in a mixing bowl and mix well. 
Divide the mixture into small portions and form into balls. 
Arrange chicken balls in a non-sticking oven tray and bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until well cooked.

Step - 2
=========

Trim away the woody end of the asparagus and cut into 4cm pieces.

Step - 3
=========

Add water, chicken balls and MAGGI Chicken Noodle Soup to a large saucepan. 
Bring to boil and simmer on low heat for 6 minutes uncovered. 
Add asparagus and carrot slices then simmer for another 5 minutes.

Step - 4
=========

Garnish with parsley and serve.
