RECIPE FOR MAGGI

1. Boil 1 1/2 cups water.
2. Put noodles.
3. Add tastemaker.
4. Cook for 2 minutes.
5. Ready to eat.
*****END OF BASIC RECIPE******

RECIPE FOR MAGGI WITH EGG
1. Boil 1 1/2 cups water.
2. Put noodles.
3. Add tastemaker.
4. Cook for 2 minutes.
5. Add beaten egg.
6. Take away from stove, and let it be for 1 minute.
7. Ready to eat.
*****END OF MAGGI WITH EGG RECIPE******
