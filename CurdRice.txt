Ingredients:
Boiled rice 1 cup
Fresh curd 1/4 cup
Fresh milk 1/2 cup
Butter
Fruits
Grated carrots
Salt
Corriander leaves

Steps to make a curd rice:
  1. Add the rice to a clean bowl.
  2. Add curd to the rice. 
  3. Add milk to the rice.
  4. Mix it well.
  5. Add butter as you wish preferabbly 1 table spoon.
  6. Add salt for taste.
  7. Add fruits and grated carrots.
  8. Garnish it with corriander leaves.
  9. Serve it in a bowl.

Recommended Side-dish
  Potato chips & fries
  Pickle
