Recipe for spanish Omlette
1. 3 eggs.
2. 5-6 potatoes
3. Rice bran oil.
4. Sauce
5. Peel of the potatoes and fry them in hot oil.
6. For making the omelette fluffy, mix it well or you can add a little bit of milk.
7. After the potatoes have been fried, add them to the mixture of 3 eggs on the pan.
8. Move it well on the pan and keep a lid on it.
9. Enjoy and do share it with your friends.
