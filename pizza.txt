

Recipe for Navin Reddy


1. Prepare the pizza dough.
2. Roll out a large round base using a rolling pin.
3. Take lots of vegies(mushrooms,capsicum,onion,tomato,etc. of your choice)or corn or paneer.
4. Cover the dough with sauces and veggies.
5. And yes! Lots of mozerella cheese.
6. Bake it in the oven at 180 degrees centigrade for 35 minutes.
7. Delicious pizza is ready.
